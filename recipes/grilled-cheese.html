The Classic Kraft Singles Grilled Cheese

This is probably one of the easiest things that you can make for yourself on a day that you don't want to make anything. It's some cheese between a couple of slices of bread. What else can I say?

Ingredients:

per sandwich:

  • 2 slices of wonderbread
  • 2 slices of Kraft american cheese
  • Some butter

Instructions:

  1. Turn on stove and heat pan to medium heat
  2. Butter both slices of bread
  3. Place the slices of cheese between the slices of bread (bread should be butter side out)
  4. Place the sandwich in the pan and cook until the underside is golden brown then flip
  5. Once both sides are golden brown and the cheese is melted, it's ready to serve
