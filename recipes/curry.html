Japanese Style Curry

When it comes to a good home cooked comfort meal, sometimes there's only one real option, and that option is classic Japanese style curry.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 box of Golden curry (or other instant curry roux)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 carrot, chopped to ~1/2" (~2cm) pieces
  • 1 potato, cubed at ~1/2" (~2cm)
  • ~1/2 a pound (~300g) of cut up chicken
  • 3 and 3/4 cups (750ml) of water

Instructions:

  1. Cut up all the ingredients
  2. Add a couple teaspoons of cooking oil to a large pot
  3. Put the meat and the veggies in the pot and fry for about 10 minutes over medium heat
  4. Add the water and bring to a boil
  5. Lower the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, skimming the surface of any foam or scum
  6. Remove from heat and add the roux blocks one at a time while stirring
  7. Once the roux blocks have dissolved, return to low heat
  8. Let simmer for a few minutes until the roux thickens
  9. Serve with rice and enjoy!
